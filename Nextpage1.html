• YOUNG ADULT AND MAJOR TURNING POINTS

At 18, we moved again — this time to Port Harcourt, Rivers State. I did a diploma there and worked small freelance gigs. In 2024, at 19, I got admitted into the Federal University of Technology Owerri, FUTO, for MSc Computer Science. Yeah, I jumped straight in after a fast-tracked BSc. It felt like coming home to Imo, but with a bigger goal. The turning point was year 1 of my MSc, when I joined a research group working on AI for agricultural yield prediction. My thesis is on that now. It’s slower than I expected. Some weeks I question if I’m cut out for research. Other weeks, a model works and I forget to sleep.
